About SRM HackerEarth

We are the official Campus Club of HackerEarth at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Kattankulathur.

We are a Club (and Community) focused at providing New Developers an opportunity to excel. We organize events to help students find their interests in different domains of technology. Stay tuned as we have a lot coming up!

Are you a Club waiting to host Recruitments on HackerEarth's Platform?

Reach out to us so we can assist you with it!
